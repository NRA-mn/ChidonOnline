STEP 1

Understand the Unit

Who: Fifth Grade Chayolim
Where: All Places
When: Everyday

Things to do 
- Learn one or two units everyday (in order)
- Use your chidon book to read along
- Answer all questions out loud
- Use the proper Hebrew terms or words when explaining (you can look at the book)

Goal: Understand the concepts and be familiar with Hebrew terms (not memorizing)

STEP 2

Review Past Units Every Day

Who: Fifth Grade Chayolim
Where: All Places
When: Right away

Things to do:
- After learning a new chapter, do the review for everything including the new chapter
- Remember details and Hebrew terms from past Units
- Understand how the mitzvos can play out in a scenario

Goal: Refresh all the infromation you learned. We want it to be a story that you heard so many time, you san say it by heart. Or like giving a test on how your house looks - you know it so well because you live in it!

STEP 3

Learning Mitzvah Boxes

Who: Fifth Grade Chayolim
Where: All Places
When: After understanding and reviewing the Units

Things to do:
- Do one set of Mitzvos a day. It should take three days total.
- You can use your own Chidon Book or Mitzvah Flashcards
- Make up your own hints!

Goal: Focus on the Mitzvah in Hebrew and give yourself hint and see connections to different Mitzvos

STEP 5

Put it all together

Who: Fifth Grade Chayolim
Where: All Places
When: After doing Steps 1-4

Things to do:

Goal: Being able to know the Mitzvah Cards perfectly and also which Mitzvah Details it connects to and also the other way around.
